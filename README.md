# **Altek Pro LLC — Handyman Website

Technical Requirements (Final Version)**

## 1. Company & Branding

*Company name (brand):* Altek Pro
*Legal name:* Altek Pro LLC

*Slogan (approved):*
*Local Handyman. Professional Results.*

*Brand positioning:*
Local, reliable handyman service in Orange County with a professional, engineering-based approach.

---

## 2. Goal of the Website

Create a *clean, modern, American-style handyman website* that:

* Generates service requests (leads)
* Builds trust with U.S. homeowners
* Clearly explains *services and service area*
* Does NOT overload the visitor with information

---

## 3. Target Audience

* Homeowners and renters in Orange County, CA
* Property managers
* Small business owners
* English-speaking U.S. customers

---

## 4. Core UX Principle (Critical)

The site must feel *simple and easy*, even with many services offered.

*Rules:*

* Services grouped into *categories (cards)*
* Sub-services hidden inside *expandable lists (accordion)*
* Minimal text on homepage
* Mobile-first design
* Plenty of white space

---

## 5. Website Structure (Pages)

### 5.1 Home Page

Must include:

* Brand name: *Altek Pro*
* Slogan: *Local Handyman. Professional Results.*
* Short value statement (reliable, professional, local)
* Primary CTA: *Request a Quote*
* Services overview (category cards)
* Service area (Orange County)
* Reviews preview
* Gallery preview
* Contact form block

---

### 5.2 Services Page

* Grid of service category cards (image + title)
* Clicking a card opens details (accordion or separate section)
* No long paragraphs
